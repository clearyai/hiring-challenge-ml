Medical endoscopes - Endotherapy device -
Separating forceps
1 Scope
This standard specifies the scope, terms and definitions, requirements, and test methods
of separating forceps.
This standard is applicable to separating forceps used in endoscopic surgery.

3 Terms and definitions
For the purposes of this document, the following terms and definitions apply.
3.1
separating forceps
a type of endotherapy device intended to be used in conjunction with specified
endoscopes, primarily consisting of a handle, forceps shaft, and forceps jaws, utilized for
separating tissues at surgical parts of the human body
