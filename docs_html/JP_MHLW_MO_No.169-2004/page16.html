pertaining to the delivery of the product to the product recipient and the business
after the product recipient has received the product)
二 Requirements that are not specified by the product recipient, but are required by
the product recipient in advance, or for the intended use of the product, and are
known to the manufacturer, etc.
三 Among the provisions of laws and regulations, those related to the said product
(IV) Requirements pertaining to education and training for users necessary for the

products
