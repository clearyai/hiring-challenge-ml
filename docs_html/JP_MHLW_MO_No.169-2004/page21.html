(Record Book pertaining to Design and Development)
Article 36-2 (1) a manufacturer, etc. shall, for each product or similar product group,
prepare and retain a record book pertaining to a record certifying conformity to the
requirements pertaining to design and development, a record of changes in design
and development, and a record book pertaining to the materials referenced in design
and development.
