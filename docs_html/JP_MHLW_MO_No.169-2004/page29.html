(Management of Equipment and Equipment)

necessary for demonstrating the conformity of a product to the product requirements,
and the equipment and instruments for such monitoring and measurement.
2 The manufacturer, etc. shall document procedures for the monitoring and
measurement set forth in the preceding paragraph in a manner that is feasible and
consistent with the requirements for such monitoring and measurement.

measurement, the manufacturer, etc. shall ensure that the monitoring and

have been prepared and maintained.
