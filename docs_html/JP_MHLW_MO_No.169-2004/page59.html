In the following cases, validation of the cleaning process and other necessary
validations shall be performed, and a record thereof shall be created and stored.
(1) When the manufacturing facility commences the manufacture of products
related to remanufactured single-use medical devices
(2) When there is a change in the manufacturing procedure, etc. that has a
significant impact on the quality of the product related to the remanufactured
single-use medical device
(3) There is a change in the quality, performance or specifications of the
prototype medical device
(4) Other cases where it is deemed necessary to properly carry out
manufacturing control and quality control of products related to
remanufactured single-use medical devices
(B) to restrict access to the remanufacturing clean area by persons other than those
who engage in work in the remanufacturing clean area as much as possible.
(H) not to allow remanufactured parts with pathogenic microorganisms or other
